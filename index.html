Pst. Prof Sunday Dada is a Husband to Pst. Dr Adenike Dada and also a father to three wonderful children (two boys and one girl), Ayomide, Joshua and Toluwa. The celebrant, Pst Sunday has been married for over 22 years and has since been enjoying a lovely and peaceful family.

Pst Sunday is a also Pastor and very seasoned minster of God's word. He preaches the word of God with great zeal and understanding such that every single person under his ministration recieves the blessing of the word. He is currently the District pastor of the Glory District of Chapel of His Glory International. He hosts several powerful program as inspired by the Holy Spirit, one of which is the Monthly Encounter With God which is held on the first Saturday of every month at the Headquaters of Chapel of His Glory, Oriapata, Opposite School of Nursing, Ado-Ekiti.

He is a also an Academic Scholar anc currently a Professor in the Faculty of Arts, EKSU. He is both deligent in this career and the ministry such that his life has become a motivation many around him who find it difficult to combine their career with ministry and be successful in both.
